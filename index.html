Apex — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026  ·  Effective date: May 29, 2026

1. Overview

Apex ("we", "us", "our") is a Formula 1 companion app developed and operated by Ruddy Marie-Joseph. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

By using Apex, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the service — authenticate your account and sync your preferences across sessions.
  • Push notifications — alert you before F1 sessions and send race results when available. You can disable this at any time from the app settings or your device's notification settings.
  • App improvements — aggregate, anonymous usage patterns to prioritise features (no individual tracking).
  • Legal compliance — retain minimal data as required by applicable law.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Apex integrates the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database & authentication. Privacy Policy
  • OneSignal — push notifications. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In — OAuth authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign In — OAuth authentication. Privacy Policy
  • OpenF1 / Jolpica — public F1 data APIs. No personal data is transmitted to these services.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure (EU region). All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2+. Supabase enforces row-level security (RLS) so your data is accessible only to your authenticated session.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Anonymous session data is purged after 90 days.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & applicable law)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection / Restriction — object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of push notifications at any time in-app or in device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@apex-f1.net. We will respond within 30 days.

You can also delete your account directly from the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Apex is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence (including the United States, where Supabase and OneSignal maintain infrastructure). These transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms as required by applicable law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify you via a push notification or in-app message. Continued use of Apex after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
